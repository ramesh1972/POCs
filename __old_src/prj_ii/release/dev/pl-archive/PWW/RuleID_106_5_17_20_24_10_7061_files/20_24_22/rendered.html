IIT-Kanpur students to go on 12-hour 'mass fast' tomorrow

Kanpur, June. 17 (PTI): Students of the Indian Institute of Technology-Kanpur have decided on a 12-hour "mass fast" here tomorrow against the proposed OBC quotas in institutions of higher learning even as their relay hunger strike entered the 21st day today.

The en masse hunger strike would be held in front of the statue of Mahatma Gandhi at Phool Bagh and if the district administration denied permission, the venue would be the Town Hall, spokesman for the 'Forum for Indians against Reservation' Rahul 'Bharat' said.

He said well-known motivational guru Shiv Khera had consented to attend the programme.

Rahul 'Bharat' said the relay fast by IIT-K students continued for the 21st day and students of other IITs and institutes of higher learning had extended full support to the movement against reservation in higher education.

The sit-in and demonstration by students and junior doctors of GSVM Medical College during lunch hours entered its fifth day.
